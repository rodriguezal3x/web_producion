THE DEFENDERS

Inspired by Crypto Currency Candlesticks and Culture, we have created 10K Bulls and 10K Bears.Every Defender has its own traits, including skins, expressions and their favorite token.

THE RARITY

Besides their appearance, each Defender holds a Candle. Just as in real charts there are 5 Candletypes, each more Bullish/Bearish than the previous one.

The Challenges

The first 1000 wallets to hodl a Defender (Bull or Bear) will be invited to a series of collector's challenges, mini-games, and puzzles. Winners will have their Defender's Candle promoted to Marobuzu.

The Burned Ones

Burning has done great to the Crypto environment. In our effort to replicate this environment, we will introduce Burning as well. During this event, Hodlers will have the option to Burn Defenders and in exchange, will be able to upgrade another Defender´s Candle of their choice by one level.

The Metaverse

If you love the Crypto Culture and Cartoon Violence, you will love our Metaverse.Some of our Main Sections will be:

Bull Moon

Bull Headquarters should obviously be located on the Moon. Here, Bull Hodlers will have access to Exclusive Bull-themed crypto art, forums, news and more.

The Bear Cave

For thousands of years Bears have lived in Caves. Our Bears are no exception. Bear Hodlers will have access to Exclusive Bear-themed crypto art, forums, news and more.

The Arena

This is the place where Bulls and Bears battle ETHernally! There will be several game modes launched. All of them are inspired by the Crypto Culture! Whales will be there too..

Graveyard

Burnt NFTs will have their own grave here. A place for Hodlers to visit and mourn the fallen ones. Both Bears and Bulls will share the same Graveyard, but obviously separated in 2 sections.

The Art LABS

All our Defenders come with transparent backgrounds so that you can eventually customize them even more. In our labs all Hodlers will be provided with Backgrounds, Filters and more to go nuts!

The Airdrop

We are switching to the 3D Era. All Hodlers can expect a huge surprise. Our 2D Metaverse will be integrated on its own section.

The Giving Back

10% of all primary sale proceeds belong to our community to decide what to do with it. Share it between Hodlers? Charity project? Your voice matters and we will let you choose.
